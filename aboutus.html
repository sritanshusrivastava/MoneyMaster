Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

+

You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot Password" on the login page, or by going to Settings > Security in your app and selecting "Change Password".

Is my financial data secure?

+

Yes, we use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption and never store your banking credentials. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and we're compliant with industry security standards.

What devices does MoneyMaster support?

+

MoneyMaster is available on iOS, Android, and as a web application. You can access your account from any device with internet connectivity.

How much does MoneyMaster cost?

+

We offer a free tier with basic features, and premium plans starting at ₹99/month with advanced analytics, investment tracking, and priority support.

Can I connect multiple bank accounts?

+

Yes, you can connect multiple bank accounts, credit cards, and investment accounts to get a complete view of your finances in one place.

Do you offer business accounts?

+

Yes, we offer MoneyMaster for Business with features tailored for small to medium enterprises, including expense management, invoicing, and team collaboration tools.
