Smart Transaction Management

MoneyMaster's Transaction Management system gives you complete visibility and control over your financial activities. Our intelligent platform automatically categorizes transactions, detects patterns, and provides insights to help you make better financial decisions.

Automatic Categorization

Our AI-powered system automatically categorizes your transactions, making it easy to track spending across different categories.

Real-time Alerts

Get instant notifications for unusual transactions, large purchases, or when you're close to exceeding your budget limits.

Multi-account Integration

Connect all your bank accounts, credit cards, and digital wallets for a complete view of your transactions across all platforms.

Search & Filter

Powerful search and filter capabilities let you quickly find specific transactions by date, amount, category, or vendor.

Key Benefits

Our Transaction Management feature provides numerous advantages to help you take control of your finances:

  • Complete Financial Visibility: See all your transactions in one place with detailed breakdowns and categorization.
  • Simplified Record Keeping: Easily track tax-deductible expenses and generate reports for tax season.
  • Fraud Detection: Our system flags suspicious activities and helps you identify unauthorized transactions quickly.
  • Spending Insights: Understand your spending habits with visual reports and actionable recommendations.
  • Easy Reconciliation: Match transactions with your budget and financial plans to ensure you're on track.
  • Historical Analysis: Review past transactions and identify trends to improve future financial decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my transaction data?

We use bank-level 256-bit encryption to secure all your data. Our system is SOC2 compliant and we never store your bank credentials on our servers. Your security is our top priority.

Can I manually add transactions?

Yes! While our system automatically captures transactions from connected accounts, you can also manually add cash transactions or transactions from accounts that aren't connected.

How far back can I see my transaction history?

MoneyMaster stores your transaction history indefinitely, allowing you to access and analyze years of financial data as long as you maintain your account.

Can I export my transaction data?

Absolutely! You can export your transaction data in multiple formats including CSV, PDF, and Excel for your record-keeping or tax preparation needs.
