Security Best Practices

While we protect your data with advanced security measures, here are some tips to keep your account extra secure:

Strong Passwords

Use a unique, strong password with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Consider using a password manager.

Enable 2FA

Always enable two-factor authentication for an additional layer of security beyond your password.

Regular Monitoring

Review your account regularly and set up notifications for all transactions and account changes.

Secure Networks

Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions. Use secure, private networks when possible.

App Updates

Keep the MoneyMaster app updated to the latest version for the newest security enhancements.

Phishing Awareness

Be cautious of emails or messages asking for your login credentials. We'll never ask for sensitive information via email.
